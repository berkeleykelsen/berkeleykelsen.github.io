About Our Society

The Berkeley Kelsen Society is dedicated to advancing rigorous and innovative scholarship in the fields of legal theory and the history of legal and political thought. Our interests span from the foundational ideas of antiquity to contemporary debates shaping the twenty-first century.

Bringing together graduate students, faculty, and scholars from across disciplines, our Society serves as a forum for reading groups, workshops, and discussions that foster critical engagement with the works of jurists, philosophers, and political theorists.

We aim to create a collaborative intellectual community where participants can explore enduring questions about law, justice, and political order, drawing connections between historical traditions and modern challenges.
